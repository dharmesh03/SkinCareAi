Tips for Best Results

1

Good Lighting – Use natural daylight or a bright lamp. Avoid flash as it washes out skin details.

2

Clear Focus – Hold phone steady. The affected area should be sharp and in focus, not blurry.

3

Close Enough – Get close to the affected area (15–20 cm away). Show the edges of the condition.

4

Clean Skin – Remove any cream, makeup or dirt from the area before photographing.

5

Multiple Angles – If one photo doesn't capture it well, try from different angles and upload the clearest one.
